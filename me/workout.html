Healthy Eating Tips:
  • 🥗 Drink 2-3 liters of water or ginger lemon tea daily to boost metabolism and aid digestion.
  • 🥗 Skip sugary drinks; opt for homemade yogurt drinks or unsweetened fruit juices.
  • 🥗 Use spices like cumin, turmeric, and coriander to flavor dishes without excess oil.
  • 🥗 Eat mindfully, stopping at 80% fullness, inspired by traditional dining habits.
  • 🥗 Include nuts (almonds, peanuts) for healthy fats and lean proteins like lentils or fish.
  • 🥗 Choose whole grains like brown rice or buckwheat flatbread and fiber-rich greens like spinach.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

About the Game

Q: What kind of game is "Matrix Defense"?
A: "Matrix Defense" is an idle defense web game where you defend your base from incoming waves of robotic enemies. Your goal is to upgrade your plasma cannon and survive as long as you can.
Q: Is the game free?
A: Yes, the game is completely free to play. No download or installation is required; you can play directly in your web browser.
Q: What devices can I play on?
A: You can play on both mobile and PC web browsers.

How to Play

Q: What is the objective of the game?
A: Your objective is to destroy all enemies before they reach your base at the bottom of the screen, progressing through waves and surviving for as long as possible before your Life reaches 0.
Q: How do I earn gold?
A: You earn gold by destroying enemy robots. Gold is used to upgrade your cannon.
Q: What types of upgrades are available?
A: You can upgrade your Attack Damage (ATK), Attack Rate (RATE), Range (RNG), and the number of targets your cannon can simultaneously attack (LASER).
Q: What happens if my Life reaches 0?
A: If your Life reaches 0, it's game over. You can click the 'Restart' button to start a new game.

Troubleshooting & Other

Q: The game isn't working correctly.
A: Try refreshing your browser or using a different one. Please also check your internet connection. If the problem persists, feel free to contact the developer.
Q: I want to share my high score with friends.
A: After the game is over, you can click the 'Share' button to share your current WAVE record.
Q: Are there other games?
A: Yes, you can click the 'Other Games' button to explore other web games from the developer.

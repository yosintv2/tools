About the Game

Q: What kind of game is "Marble Flick"?
A: "Marble Flick" is a fast and simple web board game where you flick marbles to push your opponent's marbles off the board. You can play 1-on-1 or against an AI.
Q: Is the game free?
A: Yes, the game is completely free to enjoy. No download or installation is required; you can play directly in your web browser.
Q: What devices can I play on?
A: While optimized for mobile web, you can play on any device that supports a web browser.

How to Play

Q: What is the objective of the game?
A: The goal is to keep all your marbles on the board while flicking all of your opponent's marbles off the board to win.
Q: How do I move the marbles?
A: On your turn, drag a marble to select its direction and power. The longer you drag, the stronger the flick.
Q: What happens if a marble goes off the board?
A: Marbles that go off the board are removed. The goal is to remove your opponent's marbles; if your own marble goes off, it's a disadvantage.
Q: What are the game-ending conditions?
A: The game ends when all marbles of one player are off the board. The player with remaining marbles of their color wins. It's also possible for a draw if all marbles of both players go off simultaneously.

Game Modes

Q: How do I play AI Mode?
A: In AI Mode, you (black marbles) compete against the AI (white marbles). The AI will automatically take its turn to flick marbles.
Q: What is 2P Mode?
A: In 2P Mode, two players take turns playing on the same device. It's great for challenging a friend.

Troubleshooting

Q: The game isn't working correctly.
A: Try refreshing your browser or using a different one. Please also check your internet connection. If the problem persists, feel free to contact the developer.
Q: The game is lagging.
A: This might be due to your device's performance or other apps/tabs running in the background. Try closing unnecessary apps/tabs or checking your network environment.

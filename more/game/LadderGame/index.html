Q1. How do I play the Ladder Game?
A. Tap a number at the top to start the ladder descent and see your result!
Q2. What does Level Up mean?
A. You pass the current wave and move on to a harder round.
Q3. What ends the game?
A. If your result is Game Over (X), the game ends.
Q4. Is it random?
A. Yes! The horizontal ladders and endings are shuffled every round.
Q5. How do I restart?
A. Tap Restart to play from Wave 1 again.
Q6. Can I share this game?
A. Yes! Tap Share to copy the link or use mobile share options.
